Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This app ("Pavithraa Hospital Automation") is used internally to schedule and publish short-form video content to Instagram and Facebook on behalf of Dr. Pavithraa Abishek's medical practice. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

What data we access

The app uses the Meta Graph API solely to publish pre-recorded video content to a Facebook Page and its linked Instagram Business account that we own and manage. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data belonging to third parties or the public.

How we use it

Access tokens are used only to authenticate scheduled posting of our own content. No user-submitted data, messages, or personal information from page visitors or followers is accessed, stored, or shared.

Data retention

Video files are temporarily hosted on our cloud storage provider only until they are successfully published to both platforms, after which they are deleted.
